FileSeek for macOS

Your intelligent file organization and search assistant.

What is FileSeek?

FileSeek is a native macOS application designed to solve a common problem: messy, unorganized folders. Using the power of the Google AI platform (Gemini models), FileSeek analyzes the files in any folder—based on their names, formats, and content—and proposes a clean, logical folder structure. With one click, your chaos is organized.

Furthermore, FileSeek revolutionizes how you find your files. Instead of guessing keywords, you can perform a semantic search. Simply describe the document you are looking for (e.g., "the financial report from last quarter" or "a presentation about marketing performance"), and FileSeek will find the most relevant files, even if the exact words aren't in the title.

Core Features

  • AI-Powered Organization: Select any folder and let the AI propose a new, intelligent structure with folders and subfolders. Choose between a simple 'Basic' or a deep 'Advanced' organization.
  • Powerful Semantic Search: Find documents by describing their content and meaning, not just keywords. It's like talking to your files.
  • Interactive Refinement: Modify the AI's organization plan with natural language commands before applying changes.
  • Secure and Private: Requires your own Google AI Studio API key, which is stored securely in your Mac's Keychain. Your files and your key are never sent to our servers.
  • Native macOS Experience: Built with SwiftUI for a fast, clean, and familiar interface that supports both light and dark modes.

Privacy First

Your privacy is our priority. FileSeek does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All file analysis and organization happens between your Mac and Google's AI API, secured by your personal API key. Your files remain on your device.
